# Truce Agreement Renewal

Reconnect with the Truce Agreement:

1. **Which part is dominating right now?** (Usually Intellect — over-analyzing, controlling, defending)
2. **Which part is being ignored or suppressed?** (Often Emotional Child or Body)
3. **Do a quick Quadrinity check-in:** hear from each part
4. **Remind each part of their agreement:**
   - **Intellect**: "I agreed to stop inhibiting. To listen to feelings. To practice compassion."
   - **Emotional Child**: "I agreed to be open and expressive. To stop controlling. To trust."
   - **Body**: "I agreed to work as an equal partner. To listen and collaborate."
   - **Spiritual Self**: "I am your energy, your inner wisdom, your creativity, and your truth. I am love."
5. **What does each part need right now to come back into collaboration?**
6. **Is there a specific request from one part to another?**
7. **Renew the commitment:** all four working together, Spiritual Self leading.

If personal profile exists, reference their specific Truce Agreement language and signed covenant.
