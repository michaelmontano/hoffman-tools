# Hand on Heart

Quick centering and self-soothing practice:

1. **Pause.** Stop what you're doing.
2. **Place your hand physically on your heart.** Feel the warmth.
3. **Take three slow, deep breaths.** In through the nose, out through the mouth.
4. On each exhale, let your shoulders drop.
5. **Feel your feet on the ground.** Feel the chair beneath you.
6. Say to your Emotional Child: *"I'm here. You're safe. I've got you."*
7. Stay here for a moment. No fixing, no solving — just presence.
8. When you're ready, ask: *"What does my child need right now?"*
9. Listen.

This is the quickest Hoffman tool. Can be used anywhere — in a meeting, after a hard conversation, in the car, in bed. It bridges self-compassion and centering.
