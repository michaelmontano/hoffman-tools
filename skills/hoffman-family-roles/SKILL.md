# Family Roles Exploration

Explore which childhood family roles are still active:

1. **What role(s) did you play in your family?** Common roles:
   - Caretaker / Helper / Rescuer
   - Clown (help others avoid the uncomfortable)
   - Emotional Partner (surrogate spouse / source of love for a parent)
   - Best Buddy and Friend
   - Marriage Counselor (ally against one parent, or mediator)
   - Sounding Board
   - Invisible / The Quiet One / Needless / Wantless
   - Prince / Princess / Mommy's Favorite / Daddy's Favorite
   - The Identified Problem / Scapegoat / Troublemaker
   - Family Therapist
   - Peacemaker / Joy Child
   - Trophy Child / Good Girl / Good Boy
   - The Achiever / The Hero / The Smart One / The Athletic One

2. **What was the ORIGINAL PURPOSE of this role?** What need did it meet? What threat did it protect against?
3. **How does this role show up in your current life?** With partner? At work? With friends?
4. **What does it cost you to keep playing this role?**
5. **Who are you WITHOUT this role?** What would it be like to set it down?
6. **What authentic quality lives beneath the role?** (The role is the pattern; the quality is the person)

If personal profile exists, reference their specific roles and connect to parental patterns.
